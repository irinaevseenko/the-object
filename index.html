Irina Evseenko

Khthon The anticipation of a geological life gaining the ascendant over the botanical and the biological. Tchouri A sculpture-architecture designed for the Air and Space Museum in Paris-Le Bourget. At once cave, alchemical alembic and astrochemistry lab. The Good Breast and the Bad Breast An architectural and psychoanalytical investigation about the mysterious destruction of an art collectors’ house in California. The Boogeyman A project in public space commissioned by the Middelheim Museum for an empty plinth located in Antwerp’s city park. A six-month performance adressing city branding, cannibalism and colonialism. Message from Charlotte A science fiction exhibition. A case study of the promotional language used in the rebranding of industrial cities The Quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog Initiated with the recarving of an iconic Czechoslovakian font, the project has expanded to an ongoing performance and exhibition-based narration, dealing with national identity, typography, surrealism, and divination. Museum of reproduction An attempt to reincarnate the work and life of a Polish Constructivist and alpinist from the 1920’s. Module A teenager’s creative relationship with coal-mining heritage is put into perspective to question the making of young artists. Embellissements A series of works focussing on the transformation of Parisian cityscape, confronting the urban planner with the worker.
