A monospaced version of an existing typeface by Swiss type foundry. Beautiful 'GT Super' by Grilli Type is the result of their extensive investigation into display serif typefaces from the 1970s and 80s. It focuses on the expressive and idiosyncratic nature of calligraphic motions, compelled into stable, typographic shapes. With this in mind, I have made a monospaced version of it (I worked with 'Display Light' weight) with lining figures, trying to keep the well-balanced lightness and overall impression of the original glyphs within the monospaced constraints.
