A monospaced version of an existing typeface by a Swiss type foundry. Beautiful 'GT Super' by Grilli Type is the result of an extensive investigation into display serif typefaces from the 1970s and 80s. It focuses on the expressive and idiosyncratic nature of calligraphic motions, compelled into stable, typographic shapes. With this in mind, I have made a monospaced version of it with lining figures.
